Elastic Construction Polymers

Have a look at our Comprehensive Portfolio of Styrene Acrylics & Pure Acrylic Binders.

The product range provides a comprehensive portfolio of styrene acrylics and pure acrylic binders that are perfectly suited for coatings applied to plaster, masonry, fibrous cement, concrete, wood and many other substrates. Elasto styrene acrylic and acrylic latex are also used as binders in the fiber bonding industry and as the main components in many construction, adhesive and sealant formulations.

At A.D. GLOBAL SYNERGIES PRIVATE LIMITED, has one of the broadest ranges of products based on Styrene - Butadiene chemistry available in the market today. Our range of Latices is used, among other applications, for CONCRETE MODIFICATION/WATER PROOFING.

Over the past several years, we have developed a strong Research & Development base, which has enabled us to develop, manufacture and export products and compete effectively against global players. Through our technical service team and well-equipped application laboratory, we also provide value added services to enable customers to constantly improve the quality of their final product.

Popular Products

A. D. Global Synergies Private Limited offers a broad range of products to the construction industry, from coatings to concrete admixtures, flooring to insulation, tiling to waterproofing. Our offer extends throughout the building envelope from roof to foundation, in wall systems and insulation, sealants and adhesives, concrete and asphalt, in bridges and pavement, in windows and doors, interior, exterior, cladding and landscaping. Solar Energy Solutions

With our diverse range of products, we offer system solutions for all solar energy applications. But we never rest on our laurels with our technical developments. Instead, we equip you perfectly for the future, today. Whether private customers, installers or wholesalers, A. D. Global is your partner of trust.

PVC Wall and Ceiling Panels

Our brand ELASTIC offers PVC wall and ceiling claddings which are available in a variety of decorative patterns. We aim for a dominant marketing and product leadership by offering qualitative and reliable PVC products through innovative technological features and superior customer service.

Construction Polymers For OEM/Formulators

The product range provides a comprehensive portfolio of styrene acrylics and pure acrylic binders that are perfectly suited for coatings applied to plaster, masonry, fibrous cement, concrete, wood and many other substrates.

Wood And Plastic Composite Flooring

WPC Flooring is a new type of resilient flooring, with 55% of wood and about 40% of HDPE. Wood fibers are mainly from wood cutting and sawdust. Resin is mixed wit HDPE with high qualilty addictive and high durability pigment.

Elastic PV Modules

Manufacturing and supplying quality-approved Large Size Solar PV Modules.

We are a preeminent organization that is engaged in manufacturing and supplying quality-approved Large Size Solar PV Modules. These modules are manufactured under the close supervision of our experienced professionals. Furthermore, we customize these modules on the basis of the specifications detailed by customers.

Module wattages range from 3W-300W. The solar cells are embedded in UV-resistant EVA plastic. The entire module is protected against dust and splash water (IP65). This ensures the long-term outdoor use. Each module is optically, mechanically, and electrically tested.Through regular monitoring and measurements, we ensure that all the electrical, mechanical, and optical requirements comply with the highest quality standards.

Our wide range of standard and speciality modules include:
Multiple configurations - 72, 60 and 36 cells
Customised configurations - 54 and 48 cell configuration for international market
Wide power range - from 240 W to 325 W

Elastic Solar Power Pack

Convert existing inverter battery setup into a Solar setup using ELASTIC Solar Power Pack.

ELASTIC SOLAR POWER PACK is a smart device, based on patented technology, fully automated which can convert any ordinary inverter and battery setup into solar power system. It lets you use the free and green energy generated by solar panels to power all your electrical equipment. You make huge savings on electricity bill and get to charge the batteries even during power-cut and of course help save the environment.

Convert existing inverter battery setup into a Solar setup using ELASTIC Solar Power Pack

If you own a UPS/Inverter which is in good condition with a good backup, you can take advantage of Solar by simply installing ELASTIC Solar Power Pack to utilize solar power during the day and save on energy bills.

ELASTIC Solar Power Pack enables the addition of ELASTIC Solar Panels to the existing batteries and Inverter. ELASTIC Solar Power Pack which has a built in converter and charger system, all in one, charges the batteries and intelligently controls the use of grid power.

Your power bills will reduce considerably if your main usage is during daytime. This is a major saving since commercial power is more expensive than residential power.

The warranty of ELASTIC Solar panels is for 20 Years.


Elastic Compact Solar Lighting Kit

Elastic Compact Solar Lighting kit is a complete setup to harness solar energy directly by connecting DC Appliances.

Elastic Compact Solar Lighting kit is a complete setup including a 20W Elastic solar panel, Control box having different ports for connecting DC appliances. The kit comes along with Inbuilt 18AH battery and 2 LED Luminaries. (5W and 3W). A table fan is also included in the kit.

All the devices are perfectly synced with the panel and the control unit. This product is highly efficient, eco friendly and portable.

Uses- Can be used to provide constant light at a place with no electricity. The control box comes with 3 LED indicators which helps to check Charging, Low battery and the fuse.
It is also used to charge mobile phones using the Elastic multiple mobile charger. All the appliances coming along with the unit have DC pins connected to the wires so that it can be easily inserted in the port.
AC charging is not required. Sunlight is sufficient for full charging of the SMF Battery.
Unique design. Super bright luminary and battery with a superior lifespan compared to others. Convenient & Maintenance free, Easy installation with plug and play features.

Works even when switch is turned off but the solar panel is placed under sunlight.
Works even under reduced sunlight.
Super Battery gives an 8 hour backup per appliance.

About Elastic PVC Profiles and Specifications

Perfect product for renovating your space to look Beautiful and Graceful

A. D. Global Synergies Private Limited is a leading building product manufacturer and supplier for cladding walls, ceilings and floors. As a manufacturer and supplier of finishing materials in plastic (mainly PVC) and wood composite material (a mixture of plastics and wood, also called WPC), we provide high-quality decorative home improvement solutions, offering differentiated products to meet specific customer needs. All products are manufactured to meet the highest standards.
Our brand "ELASTIC" PVC wall and ceiling cladding is available in a variety of decorative patterns ranging from laminated panels to printed panels with a huge variety of designs. Multiple manufacturing facilities in China and 24/7 operations provide the means to produce a diverse product range to serve many customers.
